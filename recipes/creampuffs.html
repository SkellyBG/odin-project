Creampuffs!

Ingredients for 20 creampuffs:

For puffs:
  • 60g flour
  • 40g unsalted butter
  • 100ml water
  • 2g salt
  • 2 eggs
For filling:
  • 2 yolks, 40g sugar, 20g flour, 200ml milk
  • 150ml whipped cream, 15g sugar
  • (Optional) A teaspoon of vanilla extract

Steps:

For puffs:
  1. Mix two eggs at room temperature.
  2. Pour 100mL of water, 40g of butter and 2g of salt into a stainless steel pot.
  3. Melt it at medium heat.
  4. When all ingredients have melted, let it boil for 15 more seconds.
  5. Turn off the heat and pour in the sifted flour then quickly mix.
  6. Turn on medium heat then stir the dough around for 2 minutes.
  7. Spread the dough onto a bowl and let it cool.
  8. Pour in the evenly divided egg mixture 3 to 4 times then mix well.
  9. Make sure to check the consistency of the batter. It should make a long “V” shape.
  10. Put into a piping bag with a 1cm round tip.
  11. Put parchment paper onto a baking tray.
  12. Make 20 dots evenly spaced out.
  13. Preheat the oven to 200° for 10 minutes.
  14. Bake for 200° for 10 minutes, 180° for 20 minutes, 160° for 10 minutes. Do NOT open the oven while baking.
For filling:
  1. Whisk 2 egg yolks and 40g of sugar together until well mixed.
  2. Add 20g of flour and mix well.
  3. In a pan, boil 200ml of milk at low heat until bubbles start appearing.
  4. Turn the heat off, let the milk cool slightly before pouring into the egg yolk mixture, stirring while pouring it in.
  5. Pour the mixture back into the pan at medium heat, stirring for about 2-4 minutes until it reaches a slightly thick texture, and large bubbles appear when not stirring.
  6. Spread into a plate, letting it cool before wrapping and refrigerating it.
  7. When ready, whisk the whipped cream to a stiff peak, then fold the mixture in.
